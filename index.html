Why did i abandon this website?

To put it simple, i think the good years of floras homepage have come to an end. After 5 years of evolving and active development i think i should let this project rest now.

You can take a look at the history and the evolution if you visit the Archive tab on the top right.

I host this page on github since the very beginning and did every single change manually, even when i used the blog feature in the past i always had to manually change the code and reupload the html file.

And i always had fun with that, i love html and webdevelopment but it consumed a lot of time to do every tiny change like that so thats why i will move to neocities.
