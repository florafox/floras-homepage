Why am i such a big fan of the Y2k era?

I simply grew up with 2000s tech and media. It feels nostalgic and kind of reminds me of times where i wouldn't have to worry about anything at all, just being able to play with my Nintendo DS all day or going outside playing with friends! I am also a nerd, so the technology from that era is also a pretty cool thing to me. I love using my flip phone and using my old 2007 windows 7 laptop. I also love to listen to my music on an old Mp3 player. Living like in the 2000s just makes me feel like me. Nowdays everythings boring, with everyone trying to simplify everything. Back then we had frutiger aero wich looked amazing in my opinion. Long story short, sure i am nostaglgic for this era, but for the most part it allows me to be who i really want to be, and how i really want to live.
