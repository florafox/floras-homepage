About Flora

Who am i?

My name is Flora and i currently live in Germany. My hobbies are playing video games, programming, webdesign reading and living in the 2000s. I'm also a furry but i try to not make a big thing out of it, since i find people doing it weird. So just ignore that.

My biggest Interests?

Music is a pretty important one to me, since i listen to it 24/7. I like Michael jackson, 2000s music in general, Lapfox trax and halley labs. Apart from music i enjoy the 2000s a lot! I own a lot of tech from that era like laptop, flip phone, mp3 player, consoles and more. Safe to say i love to use older stuff and basically live like people did in the 2000s.

Why am i such a big fan of the 2000s?

I simply grew up with 2000s tech and media. It feels nostalgic and kind of reminds me of times where i wouldn't have to worry about anything at all, just being able to play with my Nintendo DS all day or going outside playing with friends! I am also a nerd, so the technology from that era is also a pretty cool thing to me. I love using my flip phone and using my old 2007 windows vista laptop. I also love to listen to my music on an old Mp3 player. Living like in the 2000s just makes me feel like me. Nowdays everythings boring, with everyone trying to simplify everything. Back then we had frutiger aero wich looked amazing in my opinion. Long story short, sure i am nostaglgic for the 2000s, but for the most part it allows me to be who i really want to be, and how i really want to live.
